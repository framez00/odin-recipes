Stuffed Eggplant

An eggplant recipe that is filled with healthy nutrients and it is delicious!

The perfect appetizer or snack for midnight cravings!

Ingredients

  • One eggplant
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Sausage
  • Onions and mushrooms
  • Garlic (3 cloves)
  • Fresh basil
  • Tomato pasta sauce
  • Bread crumbs
  • Cheese

Directions

  1. Scoop out the flesh of the eggplant.
  2. Brush the eggplants with oil and add the ingredients listed before. Mix everything before you add it.
  3. Stuff the eggplant and top with cheese.
  4. Let it bake for about 30 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
